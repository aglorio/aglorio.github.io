Brochure for Linea Verde

I was commissioned to create a digital brochure to showcase the products of the Linea Verde produce brand. This was during the COVID-19 pandemic and they needed a way to get their greens to their customers' tables. So, it had to offer basic information about products, prices and shipping.

It was a very simple but urgent request, so I spent maybe a couple hours on it before my client had to send it out.
